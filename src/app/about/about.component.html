Task Manager

Our task manager allows users to create and categorize tasks to help you stay organized. Gain perspective and stay on top of your requirements by view all tasks in your to-do list. Our simple drag-and-drop feature also allows you move completed tasks to the done list so that you can keep track of your progress and stay on task.

Security

Work confidently knowing that your information is secure. Our login features protect non-authorized users from accessing your tasks, ensuring that your data remains confidential.

Mobile Friendly

Access Nodebucket anywhere. Our website is fully interactive whether you are viewing tasks from the office computer or on a mobile device on the go! Never miss a beat.
